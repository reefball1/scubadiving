Darin was born in South Florida where he fell in love with snorkeling in the ocean as a young child. In college he became a certified diver and while living in New Jersey became an avid wreck diver and completed his PADI Divemaster training. After moving back to South Florida, he retired from a career as a corporate executive, and became a dive instructor to share his passion for the ocean with others.

He loves opening the underwater world to new scuba divers and coaching experienced divers to become more knowledgeable, comfortable, and safe divers.
